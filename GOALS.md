# Goal

A personal site that proves Helen Johnston is a **multifaceted creative with real design taste** — something she can send to anyone (a potential collaborator, client, employer, or friend) and have them come away impressed.

## Who it's for

People Helen wants to impress: collaborators, clients, hiring managers, fellow creatives. They land on the site, and within seconds they should *feel* the taste and breadth — not just read a CV.

## What it has to communicate

1. **Design taste** — the site itself is the proof. Craft, typography, motion, restraint. If the site looks generic, the argument fails.
2. **Multifaceted** — photographer / DJ / creative director. One person, several disciplines, one coherent voice.
3. **Cool projects** — concrete work worth pointing at, not just claims.

## The structure (current)

- **Hero** — first impression, sets the tone
- **About** — who Helen is
- **A Side (Projects)** — the professional/design work
- **B Side (Creative)** — the DJ / personal-creative side (zine-collage tracklist of mixes)
- **Contact** — let's make something

## Definition of "done enough to send"

- Lives at a real, memorable URL (not a local file)
- Loads fast, looks intentional on phone *and* desktop
- Every section earns its place; nothing feels like filler
- Helen is proud to put it in a message and hit send

## Non-goals

- Not a blog or CMS — it's a curated showcase, kept tight
- Not feature-rich — taste and restraint over volume
